User Research

User Interviews

Interviewing users supplies an accumulation of different perspectives and ideas.
Questioning every feature and how a user interacts with the product
provides valuable insight on elements that have been overlooked
or features that are used differently than intended.
What worked and what needs improvement?

Competitor Analysis

Exploration on what design language or features work or do not work.
Investigating products that offer a similar service as yours
provides a comprehenisive overview of the difference between
a product that succeeds and does not.

User Stories

Similar to user interviews, user stories provide a medium to further
understand and think about how a user would interact with your product.
The stories revolve around the key features of your product.

Learn more

Cognitive Walkthrough

The idea of a walkthrough is to further understand the thought process
behind every action a user takes while interacting with your product.
It is important to realize and grasp the reasons behind each decision
made by the user in order to deliver a better experience.
It is integral, however, to frame such a walkthrough without
placing any biases or tampering the user's reasoning.
